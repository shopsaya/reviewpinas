Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

ShopSaya PH ("ShopSaya", "we", "us") is the operator of this service. This policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and how you can control it. By using ShopSaya, you agree to this policy.

What we collect

When you log in with Facebook, we receive only your name and email address from your public Facebook profile. We use this to create and identify your ShopSaya account. We do not post to your Facebook, access your friends list, or collect any other Facebook data.

When you shop through ShopSaya, we record which product links you click and the resulting cashback owed to you, so we can credit your ShopSaya wallet correctly.

How we use your information

  • To create and manage your ShopSaya account
  • To track your cashback and credit your wallet
  • To process your cashback withdrawals to GCash
  • To contact you about your account or cashback when needed

How cashback works

When you shop on Shopee through a ShopSaya link, Shopee pays us an affiliate commission on completed orders. We share a portion of that commission back to you as cashback, credited to your ShopSaya wallet after the order is confirmed in our weekly Shopee report. Cashback is verified against completed, paid orders only.

What we do NOT do

We never sell your personal information. We never share it with advertisers. We never post to your social accounts. We never ask for your Shopee password or payment details — you pay Shopee directly, as always.

Data sharing

We use Google Firebase to securely store account and cashback data, and Facebook Login to authenticate you. We share data with these providers only as needed to run the service. We may disclose information if required by Philippine law.

Your choices

You can request to see, correct, or delete your data at any time (see our Data Deletion page). You can stop using ShopSaya whenever you like.

Data retention

We keep your account and cashback records for as long as your account is active, and as needed to complete any pending cashback or withdrawals. When you request deletion, we remove your data as described on our Data Deletion page.

Children

ShopSaya is intended for users 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18.

Contact us

Questions about your privacy or data? Contact us through the ShopSaya website or the email associated with our official ShopSaya page.
