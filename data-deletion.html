Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: July 2026

You have the right to delete your ShopSaya account and all associated personal data at any time. This page explains exactly how.

What gets deleted

When you request deletion, we permanently remove your account, your name and email, your click and cashback history, and your wallet records from our systems.

Please note: if you have pending cashback or an in-progress GCash withdrawal, complete it before requesting deletion — once your data is deleted, we can no longer process any unpaid cashback.

How to request deletion

  1. Send a deletion request to us through the ShopSaya website contact option, or message our official ShopSaya Facebook page.
  2. Include the name and email address associated with your ShopSaya account so we can locate it.
  3. We will confirm your request and permanently delete your data within 30 days.
  4. We'll send a confirmation once your data has been removed.

Deleting via Facebook

Because you log in with Facebook, you can also remove ShopSaya's access directly from Facebook: go to Facebook Settings → Settings & Privacy → Settings → Apps and Websites, find ShopSaya, and remove it. This revokes our access to your Facebook profile. To also delete the data already stored in your ShopSaya account, follow the request steps above.

Contact

For any questions about deleting your data, contact us through the ShopSaya website or our official ShopSaya page.
